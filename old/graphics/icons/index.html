Icon Exports

Once your icon is done, I use the free macOS app "Bakery" to export it. This way, you'll have icons for every slot in Xcode, and they'll all be formatted correctly for each type and resolution. This ensures that no matter what platform you're deploying your app on, you'll have an icon ready to go.

Check out Bakery on the Mac App Store. Please note that I am not associated with Bakery or it's developer, Good Snooze.

watchOS-formatted icons are not created by Bakery, but can be provided on request.

Project Files

On top of the Xcode-ready exports, I'll provide you with all of the project files used to create your icon. These include the Vectornator project file, a PNG of the raw icon file, and an SVG of the raw icon file (if possible).

Check out Vectornator on the Mac App Store. Please note that I am not associated with Vectornator or it's developer, Linearity GmbH.

Important: Payment Policies

Once we're in touch, I have to evalute what you're looking for, to tell you if you're within the price tier that you've selected. If not, then we can discuss switching tiers or revising your designs. If neither of those options work for you, just let me know! I'm happy to try and find a price point that works for you, so that you can have an icon.

Also note that I don't do payments upfront. If you never end up liking my desgins, then it'll cost you nothing. Please be aware that I watermark all designs until you've paid, to protect myself against people who might try to get away without paying by using a sample design.
